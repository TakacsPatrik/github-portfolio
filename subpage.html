OCR with GPT – Serial Number Detection & Verification

Ezzel azonos témájú, modern portfólió-stílusú oldal, amelyen a projektleírás, a fejlesztési folyamat, statisztikák és a galéria is áttekinthető.

Áttekintés – Probléma

Problem Statement. To simplify the use of an existing validation tool – which takes photos of PCBs – it was necessary to automate the reading of handwritten serial numbers on the PCB boards. This step was intended to further eliminate human involvement, thereby reducing the risk of errors. To solve this task, I used the ChatGPT 4.1 mini model, which achieved the best results in reading the images during testing.

Fejlesztési folyamat

I began the development by gathering the necessary resources from the ChatGPT platform, such as example code, API setup and usage instructions, as well as the official documentation. Based on this, I created the initial version of the program, which was capable of processing image files and reading the serial numbers.

Once the core functionality was working, the next crucial step was refining the prompt. My goal here was to ensure that the model returned only the information I needed, without unnecessary additions. At the same time, I experimented with different models to identify the one that delivered the best results for this specific task.

Validációs logika (Hamming‑távolság)

During the analysis, I compared the 16-character hexadecimal serial numbers and calculated their Hamming distances. When performing validation, we already know which serial numbers belong to the given batch. For each recognized number, we run a comparison against all serial numbers in the batch, and the one with the smallest Hamming distance is selected as the correct match.

Lépések

  • Erőforrások összegyűjtése (példakód, API beállítások, dokumentáció).
  • Első működő változat: képfeldolgozás és sorozatszám‑felismerés.
  • Prompt finomhangolása, hogy a modell csak a szükséges információt adja vissza.
  • Több modell kipróbálása és összehasonlítása a legjobb eredményért.
  • Felismert szám Hamming‑távolság alapú párosítása a köteg ismert tagjaihoz.

Eredmények

In 57% of the cases an exact match was found, while in the remaining 43% there were small deviations: typically a difference of 1–2 characters (26%), and less frequently 3–4 characters (17%). No deviations greater than four characters occurred at all.
